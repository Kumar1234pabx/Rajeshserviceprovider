What We Offer

Our Services

🏥

Hospital Empanelment

Complete end-to-end support for getting hospitals empanelled with TPAs and all major insurance companies across India.

📄

Documentation Support

Assistance in preparing and verifying all required documents including registrations, NOCs, certificates, and tariff lists.

🤝

TPA Coordination

Smooth liaison between the hospital and TPA/insurance offices, reducing delays and communication gaps throughout the process.

🔍

Compliance Consultancy

On-site visits as and when required to assist hospitals in meeting compliance, NABH/ISO standards, and renewal requirements.

📋

Rohini Registration

Guidance and support for Rohini registration, an essential requirement for empanelment with most major insurers.

💊

Pharmacy & Bio-Medical

Support for pharmacy setup documentation (in-house or outsourcing) and bio-medical waste agreement compliance.
